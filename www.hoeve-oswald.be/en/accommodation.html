Accommodation

  1. Our accommodation

    Our accommodation is there for everyone: for families, social tourism, people with special needs, clubs or organisations in search of a nice relaxing break. Yes, even for companies seeking a completely different place to organise their meetings, team buildings, intensive training sessions, etc.

  2. Cosy & Comfortable

    Our brand new holiday accommodation complex can put up 24 people maximum. All spaces come with underfloor heating and passive cooling.

    Ground floor (view the floor plans)

    • Entrance hall and lift
    • Two twin bedrooms with en suite bathroom and walk-in (or drive-in) shower
    • Two bedrooms for 4 people with single beds and en suite bathroom with walk-in (or drive-in) shower
    • One bedroom for 8 people with single beds and en suite bathroom with bathtub
    • Professional kitchen with lowered worktop, induction cooker, large oven, fridge and freezer
    • Storage room with washing machine and tumble dryer
    • Entertainment room with home cinema and table football

  3. Cosy & Comfortable

    First Floor (accessible with the lift) (view the floor plans)

    • Cosy dining room with kitchenette, dishwashers, fridge, microwave oven and Sonos sound installation
    • Living room with bar, fridge, fireplace, Sonos sound installation and storage room
    • One twin bedroom and en suite bathroom with bathtub
    • One twin bedroom and en suite bathroom with bathtub
    • Terrace with wonderful views of the surrounding countryside

    Accessibility

    • All bathrooms and bedrooms are adapted for wheelchair users
    • All showers are equipped with grab rails and shower seats
    • First floor and ground floor have a disabled toilet with grab rails
    • The professional kitchen has a lowered worktop for wheelchair users to park under
    • The dining and living rooms on the first floor can be reached with the lift
    • The garden can be explored by wheelchair users as well, thanks to the paving
    • The ventilation system is equipped with pollen filters for allergy sufferers

  5. Sustainable

    Green surroundings demand sustainable buildings.

    That’s why we chose energy-saving technology for our farm:

    • Nearly zero-energy building
    • Heat pump
    • Underfloor heating
    • Passive cooling
    • Solar panels
    • Heat recovery ventilation
    • Heat recovery showers

    Garden

    Our guests are free to explore the whole garden and discover everything in it::

    • Campfire site
    • Camps in the wilderness for the kids to play
    • Farm animals

  7. Surroundings

    The rolling fields and meadows swaying in the breeze, the rows of pollard willows, little streams and woodland areas will provide you with the perfect setting to relax.

    The valley area is a unique habitat for various plants and animals.
